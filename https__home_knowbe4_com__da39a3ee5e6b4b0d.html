    Gain insight into vital password security practices such as passphrase use, the risks of password reuse, and the suggested frequency for password updates.

    Mobile Devices

    Learn about best practices for mobile device safety, such as avoiding malicious apps, careful granting of app permissions, and ensuring physical security through lock screen codes, alongside other helpful tips to keep your mobile device safe.

    Explore important social media safety tips, emphasizing vigilance against fake profiles, mindful sharing of personal details, and understanding and managing privacy settings.
