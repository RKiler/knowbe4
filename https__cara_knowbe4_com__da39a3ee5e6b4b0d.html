      Thank you for your interest in the KnowBe4 Compliance Audit Readiness Assessment. As of January 1, 2023 the Compliance Audit Readiness Assessment has been discontinued.

      New-school compliance training at a no-brainer price with KnowBe4's Compliance Plus

      Unlike old-school compliance training, which is generally expensive, out-of-date, boring and difficult to administer, Compliance Plus offers a simpler and more cost-effective way. With Compliance Plus you can use the power of the KnowBe4 platform to create and deliver compliance training campaigns the same way you deliver your ongoing security awareness training campaigns.

      Compliance Plus gives you:

      • A whole new library with fresh compliance content updated regularly
      • Coverage of legislative requirements, such as HIPAA and many others
      • New-school high-quality customizable modules
      • Short, interactive modules to keep learners focused, newsletters, docs, and posters are all included
      • Completely automated compliance training campaigns with world-class support and extensive reporting
